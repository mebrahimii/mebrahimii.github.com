Assessment

  • Three assignments, worth 50% (10%+20%+20%) of the total evaluation in the subject
  • Final Exam worth 50% of the total evaluation in the subject
  • Final Exam will consist of the problems similar to the problems included in the assignments
  • To be eligible to Pass this subject a student must achieve in the final exam a mark of at least 40% of the total evaluation of the final examination. Failure to meet one of the requirement will result in a TF (Technical Fail) grade being given if the overall subject mark is 50% or higher
  • All submissions of coursework (assignments) MUST be done through Canvas site
  • No submission will be accepted through email!

Contents

  • BigData, Cluster Computing, Introduction to Hadoop
  • Hadoop Distributed Storage System (HDFS) and MapReduce Data Processing Model
  • MapReduce applications
  • Hive
  • Conceptual and logical Data Warehouse Design
  • SQL for Data Warehousing
  • Physical Data Warehouse design, Extract, Transform and Load (ETL)
  • HBase
  • Apache Pig
  • Spark fundamentals and data processing
  • Streaming data and advanced Spark operations
  • Introduction to Apache Kafka

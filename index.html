Home

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science at California State University, Northridge. I obtained my doctoral degree in the field of data management in the Big Data Research Laboratory at Wayne State University. My research focuses on big data management, analysis and security. I have developed several metaheuristic optimization strategies for cloud data placement and workflow scheduling. I have published several research articles in peer-reviewed international journal and conferences, including IEEE conference on big data, big data congress and big data computing services and applications. I am a member of IEEE and ACM.
